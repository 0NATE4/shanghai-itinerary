Getting to/from Airport

From Pudong Airport (PVG) to East Nanjing Road:

  1. Take Shanghai Maglev
    • Station: Pudong Airport Station (PVG)
    • Ride time: ~8 minutes
    • Drops you at: Longyang Road Station
    • Price: 50 RMB one way (40 RMB with flight ticket)
    • Frequency: Every 15-20 minutes
  2. Transfer to Metro Line 2
    • Follow signs from Maglev platform to Metro Line 2
    • Line 2 goes directly through central Shanghai
  3. Take Metro Line 2 → East Nanjing Road (南京东路)
    • Ride time: ~20 minutes
    • Price: 5 RMB

Total travel time: ~35-45 minutes door to door
